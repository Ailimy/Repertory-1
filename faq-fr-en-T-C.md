>>>>>>>>> # FAQ
>

> ##  **What is Le Scriptorium?**

>>> - The Scriptorium is an online platform dedicated to writing enthusiasts. You can publish your own works and read those of other members. It's a free space for anyone who wants to share their creativity with the community.

---

> ## How do I register?
>>> - To register, simply click on ["Registrations"](inscriptions.md) on the home page.


---

> ## Can I publish more than one type of writing?
>>> - Yes ! You can publish novels, short stories, poetry, or any other type of writing. Just make sure you respect the rules of the charter.

---
 
> ## Do my works remain my property?
>>> - Absolutely. You are and remain the sole owner of your creations. We have no rights to your work and you can remove it from the site at any time.

---

> ## Is the site free?
>>> - ~~No, it's a pay site~~Of course it's free! Registration and basic use of the site are free of charge. 

---

> ## How do I add chapters?
>>> - You can add your own chapters by clicking on[ create a chapter.](create-chapter.md) However, you must register and [submit](inscriptions.md) your story on the [publication page.](create-story.md) If you publish your chapters directly, ~~you'll be banned~~ they won't be put online.

---

> ## How many chapters can I publish?
>>> - You can add a maximum of 10 chapters at a time once your story has been accepted and your first chapter has been put online by the moderators.

---

> ## What happens if my story isn't published?

>>> - ~~It never will~~ **Don't panic!** A long waiting time does not necessarily mean that your story has been rejected. We make every effort to process submissions **as quickly as possible** and update publications as quickly as possible.

> - If you would like to know the status of your submission, please contact us by `e-mail` at: [lescriptorium@gmail.com](mailto:lescriptorium@gmail.com). We'll be happy to keep you informed!


---
