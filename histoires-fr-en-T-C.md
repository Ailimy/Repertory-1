>>>>>>> # The stories:

>>>>>>> ### ✍️ Publish your writings

>>>> Want to share your own creations? [Register](inscriptions.md) and go to the publication [page to submit](create-story.md) your works. You'll need to add:

>>>>- ❇︎ The name of the work.
>>>>- ❇︎ Genre (fantasy, historical, action, horror, etc.)
>>>>- ❇︎ Type (novel, short story, fanfiction...etc.)
>>>>- ❇︎ The synopsis.

>>>> Once your story has been submitted, it will be reviewed before being added to the Scriptorium story list.
>
>>>> You can [then add new chapters](create-chapter.md).
>
>>>> Before you publish, make sure you follow the** guidelines in the** [charter](charte.md) to ensure a pleasant and caring experience for all.
> 
>>>> If you have any questions, please consult our [FAQ](faq.md).
