# About us

Welcome to **Le Scriptorium**, a platform dedicated to creativity and a passion for writing. We offer a free, open space for anyone wishing to share their work, be it original stories, authorized fan fiction, poems or essays. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced author, The Scriptorium lets you publish your stories, receive constructive feedback, and discover original works by members of our community.

---

## Our mission

Our mission is simple: to provide a friendly, respectful literary space where everyone can express themselves freely, while respecting the rights of authors. Here, you retain full ownership of your creations. Publish your writing, manage your work online, and interact with a caring community through our comment system.

---
 
## A Mutual Aid Community

Scriptorium does more than just publish stories. Our aim is to create a community of mutual support and sharing, where every member can progress and find inspiration. Before joining the adventure, we invite you to read our [Charter](charte.md) and [General Terms of Use](conditions-generales.md), to ensure a pleasant experience for all.

---

## A Community Forum

In addition to the main site, a community forum is available. This space allows you to discuss texts, literary trends or simply share your cultural favorites, whether books, TV series, films, or even food and travel! Join our lively discussions, take part in our writing challenges and be inspired by the community's creative exchanges.

## A word from the designer

The idea of creating Le Scriptorium grew out of my passion for writing. Discovering the world of fan fiction and sharing my own stories made me realize how important it is to have a space where budding writers can express themselves freely. Too often, we think of writing as a solitary pursuit, but the opposite is true: it can be a real collective journey, rich in exchange and mutual inspiration.

With Le Scriptorium, I wanted to create a community of authors and readers, a place where everyone can grow in their writing, learn from others and share their passion for words. Whether you write for pleasure, to improve yourself, or have the ambition to publish one day, this is the site for you.

So whether you're a first-time author or a seasoned veteran, a curious reader or a seasoned critic, there's a place for you here. Together, we're building a community where every feather can fly higher.

**Best regards,**  
*Sybel R. Fox*
