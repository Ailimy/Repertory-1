>>># Help with creating your story site
>
>>> Welcome to the help section for creating your own story site. Here are some tips on how to format your content:
>
---
>
>>> ## Text formatting
>
>>> **To structure your text and add headings, you can use the following Markdown tags:**
>
>>> - ❇︎ A top-level title: `#Title`
>>> - ❇︎ A second-level title: `##Title`
>>> - ❇︎ A third-level title: `### Title`
>
>>> **To create paragraphs and lists:**
>
~~~~
> ## This is a title.
> 
> > This is your first paragraph.
> >
> This is your second paragraph.
>
~~~~
>
>>> - **A bulleted list:** `-Item 1` `-Item 2`
>>> - **An ordered list :** `1. Element 1 2. Element 2`
>
---
>
>>> ## Tables
>
>>> You can also insert **tables** to better organize your information:
>
>
~~~~
> First column | Second column ------------- | ------------- Line 1 | Line 1 Line 2 | Line 2 ~~~~
